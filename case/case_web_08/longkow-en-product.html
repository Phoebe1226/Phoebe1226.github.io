Cooking Instructions

* Salad:

  1. Put 1000ml of water into a large pan, and boil bean vermicelli. Boil for 8-15 minutes. Drain well and set aside until needed.
  2. Cut the vermicelli into 5-8 cm strands and place into a big bowl. Add desired vegetables, fruits, olive oil, and salad dressing. Mix well.
  3. Add black pepper and herbs.

* Stir-Fry Vermicelli :

  1. Soak the vermicelli into water for 5-10 minutes to soften it, then drain the noodles and set aside on a plate.
  2. Add 2-3 teaspoons of oil, meat strips (beef, lamb, chicken, duck, or goose), dried shrimp, cabbage, radish, salt, soy sauce, and vinegar. Mix and stir-fry, then add water to it.
  3. Put the soaked vermicelli into a wok, stir until the vermicelli becomes transparent. Crystal clear and savory stir-fried vermicelli is ready to serve.

*Vermicelli Soup:

  1. Follow the same steps for stir-fried vermicelli but add more water (depending on personal preference).
  2. Put the vermicelli in after the soup is boiling. Boil for 3-7 more minutes and then turn off the stove. Delicious vermicelli soup is ready to serve.
